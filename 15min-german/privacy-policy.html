Privacy Policy for 15-Min German

Last updated: April 2026

15-Min German (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the 15-Min German mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication identifiers through Supabase.

User Content
If you use features such as writing exercises or language input, you may submit text. This content is processed only to provide feedback and improve app functionality. It is not used for advertising.

Audio Permission
The app may request access to your device microphone. Microphone access is used only for app features and is not recorded or stored without your explicit action.

Usage Data
We may collect limited technical data such as app interactions and feature usage to improve the app experience.

Subscription Information
If you purchase a premium subscription, payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate language-learning features
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Enforce free and premium access
  • Generate AI-based feedback
  • Improve app performance and experience
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse

3. Third-Party Services

15-Min German uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (authentication, database, backend)
  • OpenAI API (AI-generated feedback)
  • Apple App Store & Google Play (subscriptions)

User-submitted content may be securely processed by OpenAI solely to generate feedback. We do not use this content to identify users or for advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

6. Children’s Privacy

15-Min German is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date.
